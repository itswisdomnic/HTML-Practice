About Me

Wisdom Nic is a Product Designer who is responsible for the user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) of products. I work with a variety of stakeholders, including engineers, product managers, and marketers, to create products that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing by conducting user research to understand the needs of users, designing wireframes and prototypes, creating visual designs, working with engineers to implement designs, test and iterate on designs. I have a strong understanding of both user-centered design and visual design principles and i am able to work effectively in a cross-functional team environment. I possess a wide range of skills but amongst which are user-centered design skills, visual design skills, prototyping skills, and communication skills.
